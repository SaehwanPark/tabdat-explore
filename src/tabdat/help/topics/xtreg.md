# xtreg

How to invoke:
`xtreg y x1 x2, fe|re [options]`

What it does:
Fit a fixed-effects or random-effects panel regression.

What problem it answers:
How do I analyze repeated observations on the same entity over time?

Examples:
- `xtreg wage exper tenure, fe`
